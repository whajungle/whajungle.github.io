ABOUT
    galbum2 - its a simple photos albums extractor converter from
    https://photos.google.com to simple usable formats like a txt, plain
    text, markdown, html.

    no need any instalation or any deps!

USAGE
    markdown format output

        galbum https://photos.app.goo.gl/VWQ63ZYhNPAA6Maq8 -w > album.md
